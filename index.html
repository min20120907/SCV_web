Welcome to Damsel Fly

We are dedicated to creating the "Sea, Cycle, Valve" smart rainwater management system to help Taiwan and other regions address water resource management challenges.

  • Modular and scalable design - suitable for various building sizes.
  • Internet of Things (IoT) technology - real-time monitoring and data analysis for intelligent water usage adjustment.
  • Rainwater collection, filtration, and management - improve water resource utilization efficiency.
  • Integration with building facade cooling systems - save energy consumption.

We firmly believe that the "Sea, Cycle, Valve" system can bring about positive social impacts. While the Yehliu region is rich in rainwater, it faces a shortage of fresh water. This system can effectively solve the water use efficiency problem and enhance the water conservation awareness of local residents, thereby contributing to environmental protection.
